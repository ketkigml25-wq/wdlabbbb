Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Biography

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India's missile and nuclear programs.

Achievements

  • Known as the "Missile Man of India"
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna
  • President of India (2002-2007)
  • Inspired millions of students

Famous Quote

"Dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep."
